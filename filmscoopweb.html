About Us

 
 
 

The Hosts of The Film Scoop Podcast are Cash McCray and Carl Maxwell, who are brothers in law. They started the podcast in February of 2023 with an epsiode on Antman and the Wasp: Quantumania. Carl grew up watching films with his family at a young age while Cash adopted his love for film when he was 19 years old.

They pride themselves on their podcast being authentic and uncut. They give their honest opinion on films and television, no matter the cost. Because they arent the heroes that Gotham deserves, but the one it needs right now. They upload weekly episodes about new and old films and they always discuss what they have been watching that week.
